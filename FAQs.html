Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries below.

How do I register for an event?

You can register by clicking on the "Event Registration" tab and filling out the required details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 48 hours before the event.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policies vary by event. Please check the specific event terms for details.

What types of events do you specialize in managing?

We specialize in organizing a wide range of events, including corporate events, conferences, weddings, trade shows, product launches, social events, charity fundraisers, and more. We tailor our services to meet the specific needs of each event.

How much does it cost to hire an event planner?

The cost of hiring an event planner varies depending on the type, size, and complexity of your event. We offer packages that cater to different budgets. Please contact us for a customized quote based on your event requirements.

What happens if the event is cancelled or rescheduled?

We understand that plans may change. Our cancellation and rescheduling policies vary depending on the specific event and services contracted. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes, and we will work with you to accommodate your needs.
